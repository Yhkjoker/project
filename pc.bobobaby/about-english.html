bobo is a baby care products brand from Hong Kong and a leader in research and development of feeding bottles pacifiers and associated products for the last 30 years. bobo produces products ranging from bottles and cups, to tableware and home care products for kids from 0-11. all produced to the highest global standards. bobo focuses on research and development of products for infants, accomplishes excellence with technology, achieves maternal love with quality, and builds wholeheartedly a brand of baby`s products trusted by families all over the world.

  • The products have been granted 30 patents for invention, 24 for
    utility models and 41 for design.

  • Strict batch control is carried out, meeting the requirements of European EN14350-28 and US FDA, and any factor that affects children's health is eliminated from the source.

  • Products are subject to 26 procedures and 19 tests and samples are regularly sent for Swiss SGS tests.

  • All raw materials of feeding products are from Germany, Japan, South Korea, the United States, Taiwan and other countries and regions, which are supplied by more than 20 world famous brands such as BASF, Wacker in Europe and Momentive in Japan, etc.
